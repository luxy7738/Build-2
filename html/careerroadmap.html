Career Advancement - Leadership

Onboarding

1 Month
Mentor
  • Schedule an introductory meeting
  • Provide a company overview
  • Share insights into your career path
Mentee
  • Prepare a brief introduction
  • Outline career goals and expectations

Skill Development

3 Months
Mentor
  • Assess mentees' skills.
  • Recommend relevant online trainings or resources.
  • Review and discuss progress monthly.
Mentee
  • Complete at least one relevant online training.
  • Report course progress and insights.
  • Seek mentor's guidance as needed.

Project Collaboration

6 Months
Mentor
  • Assign mentees to a relevant project team.
  • Hold weekly project update meetings.
  • Provide guidance on project-specific skills.
Mentee
  • Actively contribute to the project.
  • Collaborate with team members.
  • Attend weekly update meetings.

Leadership Exposure

9 Months
Mentor
  • Facilitate introductions to tea company leaders.
  • Encourage mentees to attend leadership seminars.
  • Arrange meetings with leaders.
Mentee
  • Attend at least two leadership seminars.
  • Network with a minimum of three leaders.
  • Share key insights from leadership meetings.

Cross-Functional Learning

12 Months
Mentor
  • Arrange shadowing opportunities in different departments.
  • Discuss cross-functional projects or initiatives.
Mentee
  • Shadow employees from at least two other departments.
  • Actively participate in cross-functional projects.
  • Present findings and recommendations to mentors.

Ongoing Development and Transition

15 Months
Mentor
  • Continue to guide and support your mentee's growth.
  • Offer feedback and resources for their ongoing development.
  • Encourage exploration in new areas of interest.
Mentee
  • Reflect on your journey and achievements.
  • Discuss evolving career plans, aspirations, and new goals.
  • Express heartfelt gratitude to your mentor for their support.
